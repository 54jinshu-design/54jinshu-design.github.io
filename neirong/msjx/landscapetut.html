如何画风景速写

How to Draw Landscape Sketch

风景画是人们最喜闻乐见的绘画题材！把你看到的美景随手画下来是不是一件乐事？

画风景的工具材料和绘画技法多种多样，都比不上用速写的方式最简便！

一支铅笔或钢笔，加上一个速写本，短短几分钟，就好了！

可以只是个记录，可以只是个草图。时间多就画充分些，时间少就寥寥几笔就算完。作为速写不必太讲究，可以更随性！

用你趁手的工具，个性的笔触，画你自己的画。

我当然有我的风格特点和习惯方式，你不必和我一样！但我会向你演示基本的方法，包括常用的工具，和必要的知识，那些规律是共通 的。

同时我会向你演示常见景物的画法。		 Landscape painting is the most popular painting subject matter! Isn't it a pleasure to draw random pictures of the beautiful scenery you see?

There are various tools, materials and painting techniques for painting landscapes, but none of them are as simple as sketching!

A pencil or pen, a sketchbook, and just a few minutes are all you need!

It can be just a record, it can be just a sketch. If you have more time, paint more thoroughly; if you have less time, just finish it with just a few strokes. As a sketch, you don’t have to be too particular, you can be more casual!

Use the tools at your disposal and your own unique brushstrokes to create your own sketching.

Of course I have my own style and habits, you don’t have to be the same as me! But I will demonstrate to you the basic methods, including commonly used tools and necessary knowledge. Those rules are common.

At the same time, I will show you how to draw common scenery.
